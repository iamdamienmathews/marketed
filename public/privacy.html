Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Marketed. ("we," "us," "our") operates a marketing platform that connects clients with our agency services, dashboards, and discovery-call scheduling. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly, information generated by using the platform, and information from third-party services you choose to connect.

  • Account information: name, email address, company name, and phone number when you sign up.
  • Authentication data: a securely hashed version of your password. We never store passwords in plain text.
  • Service & booking activity: which services you register interest in, discovery-call bookings, and any notes you provide ahead of a call.
  • Channel & campaign data: if you or our admin team connects a marketing channel (e.g. an ad account) to your dashboard, we store the credentials needed to fetch performance metrics (encrypted at rest) and the metrics themselves (spend, clicks, conversions, and similar figures).
  • Communications: messages and emails sent to you through the platform are logged so our team has a record of prior communication with you.
  • Technical data: IP address and basic request metadata, used only for rate-limiting, security, and abuse prevention — not for tracking or advertising.

2. How we use your information

  • To create and maintain your account and keep you signed in between visits.
  • To schedule discovery calls, generate calendar invites, and create video meeting links (via Google Calendar/Meet or Zoom, where connected).
  • To provide the dashboards showing your connected marketing channels' performance.
  • To send you service-related emails: booking confirmations, updates from our team, and account notices.
  • To detect and prevent abuse, fraud, and automated attacks against the platform.

3. Third-party services

Depending on which services you use, the platform may share limited data with:

  • Google (Calendar & Meet): to create calendar events and generate video call links, if you or our team connects a Google account.
  • Zoom: to generate meeting links, if configured.
  • Email delivery provider: to send transactional email (booking confirmations, admin messages).
  • Advertising and marketing platforms (e.g. Google Ads, Meta Ads, Klaviyo, SEMrush, AdRoll): only if you explicitly authorize a connection so we can display your own campaign metrics back to you.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties, and we do not use your data for advertising unrelated to the services you've signed up for.

4. Data retention

We retain account and service data for as long as your account is active, and for a reasonable period afterward to satisfy legal, accounting, or reporting obligations. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time (see Section 6).

5. Security

Passwords are hashed, never stored in plain text. Third-party channel credentials (API keys) are encrypted at rest. We apply rate limiting and other technical safeguards to reduce the risk of unauthorized access or automated abuse. No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of information transmitted over the internet.

6. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and you may request a cancellation of any active service directly from your dashboard or by contacting us. Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under applicable law (such as the GDPR or CCPA).

7. Children's privacy

This platform is intended for business use by adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the relevant age of digital consent in your jurisdiction).

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data can be sent to the email address associated with your account manager, or through the contact form on our homepage.
